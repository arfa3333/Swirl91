The Story

The film follows two estranged sisters, Beth and Ellie, whose reunion takes a sinister turn when an earthquake reveals a hidden chamber beneath Ellie’s apartment. Inside lies the cursed Necronomicon — a book bound in human flesh and inked in blood, known for summoning pure evil.

When the book is opened and its passages are read aloud, an ancient demonic presence is unleashed, infecting the building’s residents one by one. What follows is a relentless night of blood, terror, and twisted family bonds — all confined within the claustrophobic walls of a crumbling high-rise.

Key Events

  • The discovery of the Necronomicon hidden beneath the apartment.
  • The first possession — transforming Ellie into a merciless, taunting predator.
  • Beth’s desperate fight to protect Ellie’s children.
  • A finale drenched in chaos, chainsaws, and gallons of blood.

About the Necronomicon

The Book of the Dead is no ordinary relic. Said to contain spells and incantations from an ancient Sumerian cult, it has the power to summon the Kandarian Demon, a force that thrives on suffering and will stop at nothing until every soul is claimed.

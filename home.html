Welcome to the World of Horror

Evil Dead Rise is a 2023 horror film filled with terrifying twists, demonic possession, and brutal survival. Directed by Lee Cronin, it reimagines the classic Evil Dead franchise with a fresh, blood-soaked twist. If you’re into gore, jump scares, and bone-chilling vibes — you’re in the right place.

Plot Summary

The story follows two estranged sisters, Beth and Ellie, whose reunion is cut short by the discovery of a cursed book — the infamous Necronomicon. What starts as an ordinary night quickly spirals into a nightmare of blood, screams, and evil spirits determined to possess every soul in their path. The confined setting inside a high-rise apartment makes every moment even more claustrophobic and terrifying.

Fun Facts

  • Over 6,500 liters of fake blood were used during filming.
  • The film pays homage to Sam Raimi’s original trilogy with subtle Easter eggs.
  • Many scenes were filmed in complete darkness to heighten tension.

Main Cast

Alyssa Sutherland as Ellie — the terrifying mother possessed by a demon.
Lily Sullivan as Beth — the tough but terrified sister fighting to survive.
Morgan Davies as Danny — whose curiosity unlocks the evil.
Nell Fisher as Kassie — the youngest, but braver than most adults.
